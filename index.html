Flight Pricing Study

A personal passion project combining Industrial Engineering and aviation. Your responses help calibrate a real pricing model.

Getting started

Welcome — and thank you for taking the time!

My name is Kamran, and I am working on a personal passion project that combines topics I enjoyed in four of my Industrial Engineering courses with my love of aviation.

The project is a data-driven dynamic pricing system that calculates per-seat costs on personal charter flights — accounting for aircraft operating costs, fuel optimization, and passenger demand — using logistic regression, constrained optimization, and reinforcement learning.

To calibrate the model's predictions, I need real responses from real people like you. You'll work through 4 short flight scenarios. For each one, you'll see the full operating costs and be asked 5 quick questions about what you'd pay or consider fair. The scenarios you are presented will adapt based on how you answer previous ones.

There are no right or wrong answers — your gut reactions are exactly what the model needs. Just go with whatever feels natural to you.

Time5–8 minutes

At the end, you'll download a small JSON file and send it to me directly. No account, no sign-up needed.
